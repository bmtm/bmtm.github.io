--- layout: default title: About Burnaby Mountain Toastmasters --- # Mission At Burnaby Mountain Toastmasters, you will learn to communicate your ideas in a fun, friendly and very supportive environment. We are all here to learn! As a member of our club you will learn how to give effective presentations that work, how to conduct meetings that sizzle, and how to motivate others towards your ideas. We offer self-paced programs and we will work with you to make sure you meet your personal objectives. # Description Toastmasters International is the world's largest non-profit public speaking organization. By providing supportive environments via over 10,000 clubs in countries around the world. We are a group of individuals striving to improve our communication and leadership skills. Both impromptu and prepared speaking opportunities are provided. All speakers are given constructive feedback.
